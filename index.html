FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about working with C2 Staffing Solutions.

Most temp and contract roles are filled within 24-48 hours. For direct-hire and specialized positions, our typical turnaround is 1-2 weeks. Urgent requests can often be addressed same-day thanks to our pre-vetted candidate pool.

We specialize in Healthcare, Education & Athletic Support, Marketing & Ecommerce, Creative & Digital Design, Finance & Accounting, Human Resources, and Fashion & Luxury. Our industry-focused teams understand the unique demands of each sector.

Never. Our placement services are completely free for job seekers. Employers pay for our recruiting and staffing services — you get access to exclusive opportunities, interview coaching, and career guidance at no cost.

We serve the greater New York Metro area, including all five NYC boroughs, Long Island, Westchester, Northern New Jersey, and Connecticut. We also handle remote and hybrid placements nationwide for select roles.

Temp roles are short-term assignments (days to months). Contract roles have a defined project scope and timeline. Direct hire means we recruit a permanent employee for your team. We also offer temp-to-perm arrangements where a temp assignment can convert to a full-time role.

Every candidate undergoes a multi-step vetting process: resume review, in-depth interview, skills assessment, reference checks, background verification, and credential validation. For healthcare roles, we also verify licenses and certifications with the relevant boards.
